Goblin.tools Task
Explore resources related to emerging technologies and information communities
  • Identify reputable sources that cover emerging technologies and information communities
  • Search for recent articles, reports, and publications on the topics
  • Join online forums or social media groups focused on emerging tech and information sharing
  • Attend webinars, conferences, or workshops related to emerging technologies
  • Bookmark or organize useful resources for future reference
  • Summarize key insights and trends from the resources you explore
Review Horizon Reports and Pew Research’s Internet & Technology studies
  • Read the Horizon Reports carefully
  • Identify key trends and insights in the Horizon Reports
  • Summarize the main themes and predictions from the Horizon Reports
  • Review the Pew Research’s Internet & Technology studies thoroughly
  • Highlight significant findings and data points from the Pew Research studies
  • Compare and contrast the insights from both sources
  • Note any overlapping themes or divergent perspectives
  • Organize the information into a clear and concise summary or notes
Identify connections between the resources and your information community
  • Determine the resources available to your information community
  • Analyze how each resource supports or connects to community members
  • Identify the key resources that have the most significant impact on the community
  • Assess the relationships and interactions between different resources within the community
  • Map out the connections between resources and community members or groups
  • Identify any gaps or areas lacking resource support within the community
  • Summarize the ways these resources contribute to the community's information-sharing and growth
Gather ideas, findings, and statistics from the resources and your own research
  • Read and review the resources and your research materials
  • Identify relevant ideas, findings, and statistics
  • Highlight or note important information and key points
  • Summarize the main ideas and significant data
  • Organize the ideas, findings, and statistics for easy reference
  • Verify the accuracy of the statistics and data collected
  • Compile the gathered information into a cohesive set of notes or a document
Design an artifact demonstrating your community’s use of emerging technologies or the internet
  • Identify the emerging technology or internet trend relevant to your community
  • Research how your community currently uses this technology or trend
  • Gather examples, stories, or data that illustrate this usage
  • Decide on the format for your artifact (e.g., infographic, video, presentation, website)
  • Create a rough outline or storyboard of your artifact’s content and structure
  • Collect or create visual and textual materials to support your demonstration
  • Design and assemble the artifact, integrating your research and visuals
  • Review the artifact to ensure it accurately represents your community’s use of the technology
  • Refine and edit the artifact for clarity, accuracy, and visual appeal
  • Share or present your finished artifact to your intended audience
